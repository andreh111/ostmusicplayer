Welcome to Castra Music

Castra Music is an anime music platform

Use your camera to scan the QR code that came with your poster,cover,or any picture material at your hand

The app will scan it right away and play back the music file for the poster

You can play the music online or you can press the download button and download the music to your device

You can save song by pressing the + button in the music player and the song is added in Saved Songs Section

You can go to the Songs section and browse all anime songs on our music platform
